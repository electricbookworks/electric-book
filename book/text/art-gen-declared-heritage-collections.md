# Specifically Declared Heritage Object/Collections

> ![](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_African_Heritage_Resources_Agency#/media/File:South_African_Heritage_Resources_Agency_(SAHRA)_logo.png)
> 
> South African Heritage Resources Agency
{:.figure}

The Specifically Declared Heritage Object/Collections listed below, represent various periods in the country’s history and are associated with various cultures and traditions:

a) The Bronze Bocarro Cannon
b) The Bronze Feyo Naval Cannon
c) The Bronze (or brass) naval cannon

3. The **Hill Organ**, at the Methodist Church, Grahamstown

11. The **NZASM locomotive No, 242**, at Pretoria Station, Pretoria
